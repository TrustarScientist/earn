EARN is the Employers' Apprenticeship Resource Network—a definitive, private-sector-led solution to Africa’s skills challenge, launching first in Nigeria.

An Operational Alliance

We are an active, operational alliance of industry architects, founded by M8 Global KNOW Network, Industry Qualifications Ltd (IQ), and leading commercial banks.

Our Powerful Premise

"Employers, in partnership with relevant government agencies and development partners, must co-create the future workforce."

We exist to transform how talent is built.

Traditional education-to-employment pipelines are fractured. EARN fixes this by providing a turnkey platform where businesses co-design, fund, and implement apprenticeship programs that deliver exact skill sets into their operations.
