About EARN

EARN is the Employers' Apprenticeship Resource Network—a definitive, private-sector-led solution to Africa’s skills challenge, launching first in Nigeria. We are an active, operational alliance of industry architects, founded by M8 Global KNOW Network, Industry Qualifications Ltd (IQ), and leading commercial banks.

Traditional education-to-employment pipelines are fractured. EARN fixes this by providing a turnkey platform where businesses co-design, fund, and implement apprenticeship programs that deliver exact skill sets into their operations. We move beyond dialogue to deployment, merging the rigorous standards of international accreditation (IQ) with the practical agility of industry and the financial engineering of commercial banking.

Our Mission

"EARN is a private-sector-led solution to Nigeria's skills challenge. Our mission is three-fold: to provide businesses with a loyal, skilled talent pipeline; to equip Nigerian youth with recognized pathways to prosperity; and to support national workforce development goals. We turn potential into productivity."
