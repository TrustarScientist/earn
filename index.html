About Us

EARN is the Employers' Apprenticeship Resource Network—a definitive, private-sector-led solution to Africa’s skills challenge, launching first in Nigeria. We are an active, operational alliance of industry architects, founded by M8 Global KNOW Network, Industry Qualifications Ltd (IQ), and leading commercial banks . Our consortium is built on a powerful premise: that employers, in partnership with relevant government agencies and development partners, must co-create the future workforce.

How We Work

Our Operational Model

The EARN model is a closed-loop system built on partnership. It moves through a rigorous cycle to ensure the result is a competent graduate and a refined curriculum.

1

Deep Needs Analysis

It begins with a deep analysis of an employer's talent shortages to identify exact skill gaps within operations.

2

Hybrid Training Design

We collaboratively build a hybrid training program, blending essential theory with applied, on-the-job practice.

3

Supported Implementation

Throughout, we administer financial support, dual-track mentorship, and robust progress tracking for every apprentice.

4

Direct Deployment

The result is a competent graduate, often hired directly by the partner, and a refined curriculum that grows smarter with each cohort.

Flagship Program

Apprenticeship Training

We architect and manage industry-tailored apprenticeship programs that combine structured learning with productive work.

Financial Aid & Incentive Management

Our dedicated financial engineering team facilitates access to learning.

Structured Mentorship

We implement a dual-track mentorship framework embedded within every apprenticeship.

Recognized Certification

EARN, in partnership with accredited TVET institutions, universities, etc. issues formal certifications that validate skill mastery.

Career Support & Job Matching

Our post-training engagement ensures successful transitions. We provide apprentices with career coaching, CV workshops, and interview simulation.

Non-Formal Education Pathways

We specialize in recognizing and upskilling talent within the informal sector. Through our Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) assessment, we credential existing skills in trades like carpentry or tailoring

Industry Linkage & Advisory

EARN actively facilitates deep connections between the worlds of work and training. We organize regular employer advisory panels to keep curriculum dynamically aligned with market shifts.

Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning (MEL)

Our robust MEL system provides transparency and drives continuous improvement.

Entrepreneurship Training & Incubation

For apprentices inclined toward venture creation, we provide a clear runway.

Policy Design & Advisory

Leveraging our collaboration with NBTE to shape national TVET frameworks and apprenticeship-friendly regulations.
