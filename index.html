About Us

A Private-Sector Solution to Africa’s Skills Challenge

EARN is a definitive, private-sector-led solution launching first in Nigeria. We are an active, operational alliance of industry architects, founded by M8 Global KNOW Network, Industry Qualifications Ltd (IQ), and leading commercial banks.

We move beyond dialogue to deployment, merging the rigorous standards of international accreditation with the practical agility of industry.

What We Do

Our Programs & Services

From formal corporate placements to informal sector upskilling, we cover the entire workforce spectrum.

Apprenticeship Training

We architect and manage industry-tailored programs that combine structured learning with productive work. Partnering with corporations, we develop hybrid curricula in high-demand fields like renewable energy, ICT, and advanced manufacturing.

Learn More

Non-Formal (RPL)

Recognition of Prior Learning. We credential existing skills in trades like carpentry or tailoring, formalizing the informal sector.

Financial Aid

Scholarships & low-interest loans via Sterling Bank.

Certification

Credentials co-branded with TVET & International bodies.

Mentorship

Dual-track guidance from industry experts & EARN coaches.

Incubation

Entrepreneurship training for those building ventures.
